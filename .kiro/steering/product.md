# Product Overview

Slide is an Instagram automation and marketing platform for creators and businesses.

## Core Features

- **Instagram Automations**: Automated DM responses and comment replies triggered by keywords
- **Deal Finder AI**: AI-powered prospect discovery and outreach via Instagram DMs
- **Cold Call System**: Lead generation for businesses without websites, with AI-generated website previews
- **Marketplace**: Buy/sell automation templates, AI prompts, and keyword lists
- **UGC Video Generator**: AI-powered video script and content generation
- **Referral System**: User referral tracking with rewards
- **Feedback System**: Feature request voting and tracking

## Business Model

- Freemium with PRO subscription tier
- Marketplace with 10% platform fee on sales
- Stripe Connect for seller payouts
- Promotion tiers for marketplace visibility

## Target Users

- Instagram creators and influencers
- Small businesses using Instagram for marketing
- Freelancers seeking clients via Instagram outreach
